How To Use It?

1. Turn it on

When you open the app it will display a shade of green in full screen.

It's perfect for chroma key compositing.

2. Record interaction

Grab a camera and record how you interact with your imaginary app.

You can swipe, tap or pinch the screen and the color won't change.

3. Add your app in post-production

Use any video editing software to replace green color with the mockup of your future application.

Make sure to add some sweet special effects:)

Features

Easy to use

Just turn it on and it's ready to go.

The screen color will display a shade of green and won't change even if you tap, swipe or pinch it.

Customizable

Just shake it and change the shade quickly.

In case you can't use shake gesture, you can still change the shade in device settings.

Versatile

It works on any iOS 5 (or greater) device.

iPhones, iPads, you name it.

Even The Icon is Green!

You can easily swap it in post-production as well.
